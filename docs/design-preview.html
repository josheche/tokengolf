06 · Glow Cards

Default (multi-color rainbow glow)

Every session is automatically tracked

No setup, no pre-configuration. TOKENGOLF measures your efficiency against a par budget that scales with your session.

  • Scorecard appears automatically on /exit
  • Par scales with prompts
  • Model-calibrated rates

Effort Levels

Auto-detected from your Claude Code settings. Higher effort = more thinking = more spend.

  • 🪶 Low · quick and cheap
  • 🔥 High · deeper reasoning
  • 💥 Max · Opus only

Technique: ::before = blurred glow layer (animated), ::after = inner card surface with 1.5px inset. Four radial gradients from corners using --glow-1 through --glow-4 CSS custom properties. Override these per tier class.
